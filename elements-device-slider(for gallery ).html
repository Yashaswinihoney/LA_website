What 2020 taught us ?

LoveActuallyME !

For better or worse, 2020 has certainly changed us. And now, looking back, one can see the lessons we’ve learned about health, resilience, and community. We learned to be appreciative of our health and even more concerned about the health of our planet.

We’ve learned how to surrender with grace, whether we wanted to or not. The year 2020 was an adventurous ride due to Covid courtesy China but gave us opportunities to slow down, pause, and revisit ourselves. It was a testing period for and taught us how important it is to live in the moment.

Of the past ten months, we spent trying to stay home and stay safe. The notion and idea of “slowing down” reached us only during this time at home; till then, we were all chasing our “dreams” and trying to compete with the other that we forgot to pause and catch our breath!

This 2020 also showed us the flip side of life; we learned the meaning of work-life balance we are working from home. It bought us closer to our loved ones and family, but house arrest also bought panic attacks and emotional disturbances.

But one thing that has not changed in these ten months is- resilience. At first, we were anxious due to the uncertainty that lay ahead in due course, and this is life, and it is not about how many rocks we hit to reach our destination; it is about how we made our way through them, and I guess, 2020 is a classic example.

2020 has made us multi-tasker and allowed us to explore our other side in a new avatar. All that matters is what we like to do and our happiness while doing what we like.

From reconnecting with our interests and family to cherishing home-cooked food and learning the balancing act of office and home, is the only positive way we could remember 2020 was. We commenced 2020 with our to-do list (and a backlog of resolutions) for the year but little did we know that it had different plans in store for us!
