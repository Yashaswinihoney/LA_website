About us

SPEC is being run under the aegis of Electronics and Communication Department, NIT Hamirpur and the department itself has collaborated in the various activities conducted by SPEC. We organise various events, workshops, and electronics competitions for the enhancement of technical acumen of students. The society holds a prominent position among various societies present in the college.

What we do?

The society also invites various guest speakers and lecturers from around India to deliver talks during the Annual Hackathon ELECTROTHON organised by us and SPEC-FEST. The technical events coupled with the fun bonanza, ELECTROTHON, the SPEC-FEST, and other competitions conducted at regular period are major hit among the students. The team has worked for the benefits of students in past and promise to continue its contribution with full dedication in future.
